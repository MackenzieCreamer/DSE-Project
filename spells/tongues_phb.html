Tongues

\n
\n
\nPHB\np283\n
\n
\n\n\n3rd-level divination\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, M (a small clay model of a ziggurat)\nDuration: 1 hour\n
\n

This spell grants the creature you touch the ability to understand any spoken language it hears. Moreover, when the target speaks, any creature that knows at least one language and can hear the target understands what it says.

Classes: Bard, Cleric, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter
Races: Gnome (Mark of Scribing)
Feats: Dragonmark of Scribing, Outlands Envoy
\nSource: PHB, page 283. Available in the SRD.\n'

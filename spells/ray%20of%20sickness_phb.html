Ray of Sickness

\n
\n
\nPHB\np271\n
\n
\n\n\n1st-level necromancy\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: 60 feet\nComponents: V, S\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

A ray of sickening greenish energy lashes out toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 2d8 poison damage and must make a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, it is also poisoned until the end of your next turn.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Alchemist Artificer, Arcane Trickster Rogue, Death Cleric, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Lunar (Full Moon) Sorcerer, Undying Warlock
Races: Tiefling (Baalzebul), Tiefling (Baalzebul)
Backgrounds: Golgari Agent

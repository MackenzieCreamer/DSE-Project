Magic Weapon

\n
\n
\nPHB\np257\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level transmutation\nCasting Time: 1 bonus action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, S\nDuration: Concentration, up to 1 hour\n
\n

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.

Classes: Artificer, Paladin, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcana Cleric, Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Forge Cleric, Glory Paladin, War Cleric, Stone (UA) Sorcerer, Hexblade (UA) Warlock, Zeal (PSA) Cleric
Optional/Variant Classes: Ranger, Sorcerer
Races: Human (Mark of Making)
Feats: Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes, Dragonmark of Making
\nSource: PHB, page 257. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.\n'

Aura of Vitality

\n
\n
\nPHB\np216\n
\n
\n\n\n3rd-level evocation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Self (30-foot radius)\nComponents: V\nDuration: Concentration, up to 1 minute\n
\n

Healing energy radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. You can use a bonus action to cause one creature in the aura (including you) to regain 2d6 hit points.

Classes: Paladin
Subclasses: Battle Smith Artificer, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Crown Paladin, Twilight Cleric
Optional/Variant Classes: Cleric, Druid
Races: Halfling (Mark of Healing)

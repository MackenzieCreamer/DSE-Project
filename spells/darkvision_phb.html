Darkvision

\n
\n
\nPHB\np230\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level transmutation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, S, M (either a pinch of dried carrot or an agate)\nDuration: 8 hours\n
\n

You touch a willing creature to grant it the ability to see in the dark. For the duration, that creature has darkvision out to a range of 60 feet.

Classes: Artificer, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Shadow Monk
Feats: Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes

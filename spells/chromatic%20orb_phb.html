Chromatic Orb

\n
\n
\nPHB\np221\n
\n
\n\n\n1st-level evocation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: 90 feet\nComponents: V, S, M (a diamond worth at least 50 gp)\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

You hurl a 4-inch-diameter sphere of energy at a creature that you can see within range. You choose acid, cold, fire, lightning, poison, or thunder for the type of orb you create, and then make a ranged spell attack against the target. If the attack hits, the creature takes 3d8 damage of the type you chose.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Watchers (UA) Paladin
Backgrounds: Prismari Student
Feats: Aberrant Dragonmark, Divinely Favored, Initiate of High Sorcery, Magic Initiate, Strixhaven Initiate

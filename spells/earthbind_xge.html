Earthbind

\n
\n
\nXGE\np154\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level transmutation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: 300 feet\nComponents: V\nDuration: Concentration, up to 1 minute\n
\n

Choose one creature you can see within range. Yellow strips of magical energy loop around the creature. The target must succeed on a Strength saving throw, or its flying speed (if any) is reduced to 0 feet for the spell\'s duration. An airborne creature affected by this spell safely descends at 60 feet per round until it reaches the ground or the spell ends.

Classes: Druid, Sorcerer, Warlock, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter
Feats: Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes

See Invisibility

\n
\n
\nPHB\np274\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level divination\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Self\nComponents: V, S, M (a pinch of talc and a small sprinkling of powdered silver)\nDuration: 1 hour\n
\n

For the duration, you see invisible creatures and objects as if they were visible, and you can see into the Ethereal Plane. Ethereal creatures and objects appear ghostly and translucent.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Watchers Paladin, Twilight Cleric, Fate (UA) Cleric
Races: Half-Elf (Variant; Mark of Detection)
Feats: Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the White Robes, Adept of the White Robes

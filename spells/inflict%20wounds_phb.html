Inflict Wounds

\n
\n
\nPHB\np253\n
\n
\n\n\n1st-level necromancy\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, S\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 necrotic damage.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Cleric
Subclasses: Divine Soul Sorcerer, Oathbreaker Paladin
Backgrounds: Witherbloom Student
Feats: Divinely Favored, Divinely Favored, Divinely Favored, Magic Initiate, Shadow Touched, Shadow Touched, Strixhaven Initiate

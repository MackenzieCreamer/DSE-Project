Stone Shape

\n
\n
\nPHB\np278\n
\n
\n\n\n4th-level transmutation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, S, M (soft clay, which must be worked into roughly the desired shape of the stone object)\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

You touch a stone object of Medium size or smaller or a section of stone no more than 5 feet in any dimension and form it into any shape that suits your purpose. So, for example, you could shape a large rock into a weapon, idol, or coffer, or make a small passage through a wall, as long as the wall is less than 5 feet thick. You could also shape a stone door or its frame to seal the door shut. The object you create can have up to two hinges and a latch, but finer mechanical detail isn\'t possible.

Classes: Artificer, Cleric, Druid, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Land (Mountain) Druid, Land (Underdark) Druid, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Genie (Dao) Warlock, Lorehold (UA) Bard, Lorehold (UA) Warlock, Lorehold (UA) Wizard, Quandrix (UA) Sorcerer, Quandrix (UA) Wizard
Races: Human (Mark of Making)
Backgrounds: Lorehold Student

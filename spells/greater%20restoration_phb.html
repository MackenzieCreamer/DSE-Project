Greater Restoration

\n
\n
\nPHB\np246\n
\n
\n\n\n5th-level abjuration\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, S, M (diamond dust worth at least 100 gp, which the spell consumes)\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

You imbue a creature you touch with positive energy to undo a debilitating effect. You can reduce the target\'s exhaustion level by one, or end one of the following effects on the target:

  • One effect that charmed or petrified the target
  • One curse, including the target\'s attunement to a cursed magic item
  • Any reduction to one of the target\'s ability scores
  • One effect reducing the target\'s hit point maximum
Classes: Artificer, Bard, Cleric, Druid
Subclasses: Clockwork Soul Sorcerer, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Peace Cleric, Celestial Warlock, Love (UA) Cleric, Witherbloom (UA) Druid, Witherbloom (UA) Warlock, Unity (UA) Cleric
Optional/Variant Classes: Ranger
Races: Halfling (Mark of Healing)
Backgrounds: Witherbloom Student
\nSource: PHB, page 246. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.\n'

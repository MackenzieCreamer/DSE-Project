Aura of Life

\n
\n
\nPHB\np216\n
\n
\n\n\n4th-level abjuration\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Self (30-foot radius)\nComponents: V\nDuration: Concentration, up to 10 minutes\n
\n

Life-preserving energy radiates from you in an aura with a 30-foot radius. Until the spell ends, the aura moves with you, centered on you. Each nonhostile creature in the aura (including you) has resistance to necrotic damage, and its hit point maximum can\'t be reduced. In addition, a nonhostile, living creature regains 1 hit point when it starts its turn in the aura with 0 hit points.

Classes: Paladin
Subclasses: Wildfire Druid, Divine Soul Sorcerer, Undying Warlock, Twilight Cleric, Solidarity (PSA) Cleric
Optional/Variant Classes: Cleric
Races: Halfling (Mark of Healing), Human (Mark of Handling)
Backgrounds: Selesnya Initiate

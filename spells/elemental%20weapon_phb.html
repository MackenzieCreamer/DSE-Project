Elemental Weapon

\n
\n
\nPHB\np237\n
\n
\n\n\n3rd-level transmutation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, S\nDuration: Concentration, up to 1 hour\n
\n

A nonmagical weapon you touch becomes a magic weapon. Choose one of the following damage types: acid, cold, fire, lightning, or thunder. For the duration, the weapon has a +1 bonus to attack rolls and deals an extra 1d4 damage of the chosen type when it hits.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th or 6th level, the bonus to attack rolls increases to +2 and the extra damage increases to 2d4. When you use a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3 and the extra damage increases to 3d4.

Classes: Artificer, Paladin
Subclasses: Forge Cleric, Hexblade Warlock, Stone (UA) Sorcerer
Optional/Variant Classes: Druid, Ranger
Races: Human (Mark of Making)
Backgrounds: Izzet Engineer
\nSource: PHB, page 237\n'

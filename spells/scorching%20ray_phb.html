Scorching Ray

\n
\n
\nPHB\np273\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level evocation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: 120 feet\nComponents: V, S\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

You create three rays of fire and hurl them at targets within range. You can hurl them at one target or several.

Make a ranged spell attack for each ray. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 fire damage.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you create one additional ray for each slot level above 2nd.

Classes: Sorcerer, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Artillerist Artificer, Wildfire Druid, Eldritch Knight Fighter, Light Cleric, Fiend Warlock, Genie (Efreeti) Warlock
Backgrounds: Boros Legionnaire
Feats: Adept of the Black Robes

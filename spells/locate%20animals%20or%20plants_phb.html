Locate Animals or Plants

\n
\n
\nPHB\np256\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level divination (ritual)\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Self\nComponents: V, S, M (a bit of fur from a bloodhound)\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

Describe or name a specific kind of beast or plant. Concentrating on the voice of nature in your surroundings, you learn the direction and distance to the closest creature or plant of that kind within 5 miles, if any are present.

Classes: Bard, Druid, Ranger
Subclasses: Wildfire (UA) Druid
Races: Half-Orc (Variant; Mark of Finding), Human (Variant; Mark of Finding)
Feats: Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the White Robes, Adept of the White Robes
\nSource: PHB, page 256. Available in the SRD.\n'

Pass without Trace

\n
\n
\nPHB\np264\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level abjuration\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Self\nComponents: V, S, M (ashes from a burned leaf of mistletoe and a sprig of spruce)\nDuration: Concentration, up to 1 hour\n
\n

A veil of shadows and silence radiates from you, masking you and your companions from detection. For the duration, each creature you choose within 30 feet of you (including you) has a +10 bonus to Dexterity (Stealth) checks and can\'t be tracked except by magical means. A creature that receives this bonus leaves behind no tracks or other traces of its passage.

Classes: Druid, Ranger
Subclasses: Land (Grassland) Druid, Trickery Cleric, Shadow Monk
Races: Elf (Mark of Shadow), Genasi (Earth), Genasi (Earth), Human (Mark of Passage)

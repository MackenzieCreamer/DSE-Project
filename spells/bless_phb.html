Bless

\n
\n
\nPHB\np219\n
\n
\n\n\n1st-level enchantment\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: 30 feet\nComponents: V, S, M (a sprinkling of holy water)\nDuration: Concentration, up to 1 minute\n
\n

You bless up to three creatures of your choice within range. Whenever a target makes an attack roll or a saving throw before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the attack roll or saving throw.

At Higher Levels.

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

Classes: Cleric, Paladin
Subclasses: Divine Soul Sorcerer, Life Cleric, Onomancy (UA) Wizard, Solidarity (PSA) Cleric
Feats: Divinely Favored, Divinely Favored, Divinely Favored, Fey Touched, Fey Touched, Magic Initiate, Strixhaven Initiate

Create Spelljamming Helm

\n
\n
\nAAG\np22\n
\n
\n\n\n5th-level transmutation\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Touch\nComponents: V, S, M (a crystal rod worth at least 5,000 gp, which the spell consumes)\nDuration: Instantaneous\n
\n

Holding the rod used in the casting of the spell, you touch a Large or smaller chair that is unoccupied. The rod disappears, and the chair is transformed into a spelljamming helm.

Classes: Artificer, Wizard
Note: Both the Fighter (Eldritch Knight) and the Rogue (Arcane Trickster) spell lists include all Wizard spells. Spells of 5th level or higher may be cast with the aid of a spell scroll or similar.

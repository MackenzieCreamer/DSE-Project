Skywrite

\n
\n
\nXGE\np165\n
\n
\n\n\n2nd-level transmutation (ritual)\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: Sight\nComponents: V, S\nDuration: Concentration, up to 1 hour\n
\n

You cause up to ten words to form in a part of the sky you can see. The words appear to be made of cloud and remain in place for the spell\'s duration. The words dissipate when the spell ends. A strong wind can disperse the clouds and end the spell early.

Classes: Artificer, Bard, Druid, Wizard
Subclasses: Arcane Trickster Rogue, Eldritch Knight Fighter
Feats: Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes, Adept of the Red Robes

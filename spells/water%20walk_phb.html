Water Walk

\n
\n
\nPHB\np287\n
\n
\n\n\n3rd-level transmutation (ritual)\nCasting Time: 1 action\nRange: 30 feet\nComponents: V, S, M (a piece of cork)\nDuration: 1 hour\n
\n

This spell grants the ability to move across any liquid surface\xe2\x80\x94such as water, acid, mud, snow, quicksand, or lava\xe2\x80\x94as if it were harmless solid ground (creatures crossing molten lava can still take damage from the heat). Up to ten willing creatures you can see within range gain this ability for the duration.

If you target a creature submerged in a liquid, the spell carries the target to the surface of the liquid at a rate of 60 feet per round.

Classes: Artificer, Cleric, Druid, Ranger, Sorcerer
Subclasses: Land (Coast) Druid, Land (Swamp) Druid, Divine Soul Sorcerer
Races: Genasi (Water), Triton
Backgrounds: Prismari Student
\nSource: PHB, page 287. Available in the SRD.\n'

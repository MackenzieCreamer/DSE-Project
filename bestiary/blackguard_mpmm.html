Actions

Multiattack.

The blackguard makes three attacks, using Glaive, Shortbow, or both.

Glaive.

Melee Weapon Attack: +7+1d6 + 4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 9 (1d10 + 4) slashing damage plus 9 (2d8) necrotic damage.

Shortbow.

Ranged Weapon Attack: +3+1d6 + 0 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target. Hit: 5 (1d6 + 2) piercing damage.

Dreadful Aspect (Recharges after a Short or Long Rest).

Each enemy within 30 feet of the blackguard must succeed on a DC 13+1d6 + 10 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of the blackguard for 1 minute. If a frightened target ends its turn more than 30 feet away from the blackguard, the target can repeat the saving throw, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Spellcasting.

The blackguard casts one of the following spells, using Charisma as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13+1d6 + 10):

  • 2/day each: command, dispel magic, find steed

Bonus Actions

Smite.

Immediately after the blackguard hits a target with an attack roll, the blackguard can force that target to make a DC 13+1d6 + 10 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the target suffers one of the following effects of the blackguard\'s choice:

Blind.

The target is blinded for 1 minute. The blinded target can repeat the save at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.

Shove.

The target is pushed up to 10 feet away and knocked prone.

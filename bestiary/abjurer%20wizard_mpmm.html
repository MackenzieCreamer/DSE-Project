Arcane Burst.

Melee or Ranged Spell Attack: +8+1d8 + 4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 120 ft., one target. Hit: 20 (3d10 + 4) force damage.

Force Blast.

Each creature in a 20-foot cube originating from the abjurer must make a DC 16+1d8 + 12 Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 36 (8d8) force damage and is pushed up to 10 feet away from the abjurer. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage and isn\'t pushed.

Spellcasting.

The abjurer casts one of the following spells, using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 16+1d8 + 12):

  • At will: dancing lights, mage hand, message, prestidigitation

  • 2/day each: dispel magic, lightning bolt, mage armor

  • 1/day each: arcane lock, banishment, globe of invulnerability, invisibility, wall of force

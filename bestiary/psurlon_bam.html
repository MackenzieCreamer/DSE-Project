Magic can\'t read the psurlon\'s thoughts or put the psurlon to sleep.

Actions

Multiattack.

The psurlon makes one Bite attack and two Claw attacks.

Bite.

Melee Weapon Attack: +4+1d4 + 2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. Hit: 6 (1d8 + 2) piercing damage.

Claw.

Melee Weapon Attack: +4+1d4 + 2 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 4 (1d4 + 2) slashing damage.

Psychic Crush.

The psurlon targets one creature it can see within 120 feet of itself. The target must make a DC 13+1d4 + 11 Wisdom saving throw, taking 14 (2d10 + 3) psychic damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

Spellcasting (Psionics).

The psurlon casts one of the following spells, requiring no spell components and using Intelligence as the spellcasting ability (spell save DC 13+1d4 + 11):

  • 2/day each: disguise self, mage armor (self only)

  • 1/day: suggestion

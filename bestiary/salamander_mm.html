Heated Body.

A creature that touches the salamander or hits it with a melee attack while within 5 feet of it takes 7 (2d6) fire damage.

Heated Weapons.

Any metal melee weapon the salamander wields deals an extra 3 (1d6) fire damage on a hit (included in the attack).

Actions

Multiattack.

The salamander makes two attacks: one with its spear and one with its tail.

Spear.

Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack: +7+1d6 + 4 to hit, reach 5 ft. or range 20/60 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) piercing damage, or 13 (2d8 + 4) piercing damage if used with two hands to make a melee attack, plus 3 (1d6) fire damage.

Tail.

Melee Weapon Attack: +7+1d6 + 4 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. Hit: 11 (2d6 + 4) bludgeoning damage plus 7 (2d6) fire damage, and the target is grappled (escape DC 14+1d6 + 11). Until this grapple ends, the target is restrained, the salamander can automatically hit the target with its tail, and the salamander can\'t make tail attacks against other targets.

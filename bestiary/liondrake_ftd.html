Blood-Chilling Roar (Recharge 4\xe2\x80\x936).

The liondrake lets out a terrifying roar audible out to 300 feet. Any creature within 30 feet of the liondrake that can hear its roar must succeed on a DC 14+1d6 + 11 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of the liondrake for 1 minute. A creature that fails the save by 5 or more is also paralyzed for the same duration. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success. If a target\'s saving throw is successful or the effect ends for it, the target is immune to this liondrake\'s Blood-Chilling Roar for the next 24 hours.

The swarm can climb difficult surfaces, including upside down on ceilings, without needing to make an ability check.

Swarm.

The swarm can occupy another creature\'s space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny neogi hatchling. The swarm can\'t regain hit points or gain temporary hit points.

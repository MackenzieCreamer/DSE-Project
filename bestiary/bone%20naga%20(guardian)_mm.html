Spellcasting.

The naga is a 5th-level spellcaster (spell save DC 12+1d4 + 10, +4+1d4 + 2 to hit with spell attacks) that needs only verbal components to cast its spells. Its spellcasting ability is Wisdom, and it has the following cleric spells prepared:

  • Cantrips (at will): mending, sacred flame, thaumaturgy

  • 1st level (4 slots): command, shield of faith

  • 2nd level (3 slots): calm emotions, hold person

  • 3rd level (2 slots): bestow curse
